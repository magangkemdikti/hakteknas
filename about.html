Who we are

Hi there!

We’re Nicole & Andreas, and we founded Meetco to energize the global conference landscape and bring curious, creative minds together. We truly love this community — it’s filled with open-hearted, thoughtful individuals who inspire one another and build meaningful, lasting connections.

Our history

Meetco started with a love for connection and creativity. From a small local idea, it grew into a global platform for events, collaboration, and shared ideas.

Our mission

Our mission is to create meaningful conference experiences that connect, inspire, and empower people from around the world. We aim to ideas.

Our vision

Our vision is to shape a world where meaningful conversations, shared ideas, and diverse perspectives come together to drive growth, spark innovation.
