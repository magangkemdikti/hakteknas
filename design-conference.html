faq

Your Questions Answered

What’s included in each pass?

Starter is best for beginners, Professional includes workshops, and VIP gives you the full premium experience.

Can I upgrade my pass later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time before the event by contacting support or through your ticket dashboard.

Are meals provided?

Light refreshments are included in all passes. Professional and VIP passes include premium meals.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, all keynote and panel sessions will be recorded. Recordings will be available for Professional and VIP holders.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes, tickets are transferable up to 7 days before the summit. Contact support for help.

Where is the summit venue?

The summit will be held at the [Convention Center Name], in [City, State]. Full details will be emailed to you after registration.
